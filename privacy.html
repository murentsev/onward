Privacy Policy — Onward

Effective date: June 23, 2026

Onward ("the app", "we") is built privacy-first. This policy explains what we do — and mostly don't do — with your information.

We do not collect your data

Onward has no user accounts and no sign-up. All information you enter — the habit you're quitting, your quit date, spending, reasons, and progress — is stored only on your device. We do not transmit it to us or to anyone else. We cannot see it.

No tracking, no ads, no third parties

Onward contains no advertising, no analytics, and no third-party tracking SDKs. We do not build a profile of you and we do not share or sell any information, because we don't collect any.

Notifications

If you allow notifications, the app schedules local reminders (daily check-ins and milestone congratulations) directly on your device. These are generated locally and are not sent through any server.

Subscriptions

Onward Pro subscriptions are processed by Apple through the App Store. Apple handles payment and billing under Apple's own privacy policy; we never receive your payment details. We only see an anonymous, on-device signal of whether a valid subscription is active.

Children

Onward is not directed to children under 13 and does not knowingly collect any information from them. As noted above, the app collects no personal data from anyone.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, we will update the effective date above and post the new version at this URL.

Questions about privacy? Email onward.help@icloud.com.
