Common questions

How do I change my quit date or habit?
Open the app, tap the “···” menu on the main screen, choose Settings, and edit your quit date, reasons, or numbers there.

I relapsed — what do I do?
That's part of the journey. Tap the “···” menu and choose “I relapsed”. Your streak restarts but your best record is kept.

How do I manage or cancel my subscription?
Subscriptions are managed by Apple: open the iOS Settings app, tap your name, then Subscriptions, and select Onward.

Is my data private?
Yes. Everything stays on your device. We collect nothing. See our Privacy Policy.
